Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our security services

What services does IslandShield Security offer?

+

We offer comprehensive security solutions including CCTV installation and monitoring, security personnel services, event security, and 24/7 emergency response. Our services are tailored to meet residential, commercial, and industrial security needs.

How long has IslandShield been in business?

+

IslandShield Security has been protecting properties in Grenada for over 15 years since 2009. We've built our reputation on reliability, professionalism, and cutting-edge security technology.

Are your security personnel licensed and trained?

+

Yes, all our security personnel are fully licensed by the Government of Grenada, professionally trained, and undergo thorough background checks. We maintain ongoing training programs to ensure our team stays current with the latest security protocols and techniques.

How quickly can you install a CCTV system?

+

Installation time varies based on the size and complexity of your property. A typical residential installation takes 1-2 days, while commercial installations may take 3-5 days. We provide a detailed timeline during your free consultation.

Can I access my security cameras remotely?

+

Absolutely! All our CCTV systems include remote access capabilities. You can view live feeds and recorded footage from anywhere using your smartphone, tablet, or computer through our secure mobile app and web portal.

Do you provide armed security guards?

+

Yes, we offer both armed and unarmed security personnel depending on your specific needs and risk assessment. All armed guards are specially licensed and trained in firearms safety and security protocols.

What areas do you serve?

+

We serve all parishes in Grenada including St. George, St. Andrew, St. David, St. John, St. Mark, St. Patrick, and Carriacou & Petite Martinique. Our 24/7 emergency response covers the entire island.

How much does a CCTV system cost?

+

Pricing varies based on the number of cameras, equipment quality, and installation complexity. Basic residential systems start around $1,500, while comprehensive commercial systems can range from $5,000 to $20,000+. We offer free consultations and custom quotes.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for both equipment installation and ongoing monitoring services. Contact us to discuss options that fit your budget.

Is there a monthly monitoring fee?

+

Our 24/7 professional monitoring service starts at $50/month for basic plans and varies based on the level of service and number of cameras. We also offer self-monitoring options at no additional cost.

Do you provide free quotes?

+

Yes, we provide free security assessments and detailed quotes for all our services. Our team will visit your property, assess your security needs, and provide a comprehensive proposal at no cost or obligation.

What happens if the power goes out?

+

Our systems include battery backup that keeps your cameras and recording devices operational during power outages. For extended protection, we can integrate your system with generators or UPS systems.

How long is video footage stored?

+

Standard storage keeps 30 days of footage. We offer cloud storage options for extended retention (60, 90, or 180 days) and local storage solutions with customizable retention periods based on your needs.

Can I integrate the system with my smart home?

+

Yes, our systems are compatible with popular smart home platforms including Alexa, Google Home, and Apple HomeKit. We can integrate your security system with existing smart home devices for comprehensive automation.

What kind of warranty do you offer?

+

All equipment comes with manufacturer warranties (typically 1-3 years). Our installation work is guaranteed for 12 months, and we offer extended warranty and maintenance packages for ongoing peace of mind.

Do cameras work at night?

+

Yes, all our cameras feature advanced infrared night vision technology, providing clear footage even in complete darkness. Commercial-grade cameras can see up to 100 feet in zero-light conditions.
